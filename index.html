About CREST
Developing Colorado's
Referee Community
C.R.E.S.T. — Colorado Referee Education & Supplemental Training — is a referee development program created under the Colorado Soccer Association to elevate the quality of officiating across the state.

The CREST tool suite puts professional-grade tools in the hands of every referee coach, coordinator and official in Colorado. Built for the field. Built for the sideline. Built for the love of the game.

Every tool is free, open source, and works offline — because great referee development shouldn't require a subscription.
