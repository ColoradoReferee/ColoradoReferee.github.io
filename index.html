CREST Team Apps
One App. Every Club.

At the start of each season, CSA provides every Colorado club with their own personalized CREST Team app — branded with the club's name, colors and logo. Tap a club below to see a live preview.

Each team app serves three audiences in one place: Coach tools, Player development, and Parent education — plus the referee recruitment pathway built right in.

🛑 RAP — Referee Abuse Prevention

Every CREST Team app includes direct access to the CSA Referee Abuse Prevention program. Coaches, players and parents can report incidents instantly from the field.

COLORADO SOCCER ASSOCIATION
Built for Colorado.
Ready for the Nation.
C.R.E.S.T. — Colorado Referee Education & Supplemental Training — is a referee development program created in partnership with the Colorado Soccer Association to elevate the quality of officiating across the state.

Every tool is free, works offline, and installs on any phone — because great referee development shouldn't require a subscription. Built by ref coaches, for ref coaches, with the full backing of CSA.

"The goal is simple — give every referee coach in Colorado professional-grade tools, give every club a way to educate their community, and give every player a path into officiating. CREST is how we get there."

— CREST Program, Colorado Soccer Association
