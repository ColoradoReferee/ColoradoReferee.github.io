CREST Team Apps
One App.
Every Club.

At the start of each season, CSA provides every Colorado club with their own personalized CREST Team app — branded with the club's name, colors and logo.

Each team app serves three audiences in one place with a simple tab layout: Coach tools for the technical staff, Player resources for development, and Parent education for the sidelines.

No app stores. No subscriptions. Just a link that installs instantly on any phone and works offline at any field in Colorado.

⚡ The Recruitment Engine

Every CREST Team app includes a referee recruitment pathway — giving players a direct, engaging route into officiating. More clubs. More exposure. More referees for Colorado.

COLORADO SOCCER ASSOCIATION
Built for Colorado.
Ready for the Nation.
C.R.E.S.T. — Colorado Referee Education & Supplemental Training — is a referee development program created in partnership with the Colorado Soccer Association to elevate the quality of officiating across the state.

Every tool in the CREST suite is free, works offline, and installs on any phone — because great referee development shouldn't require a subscription or a laptop. Built by a ref coach, for ref coaches, with the full backing of CSA's Director of Referee Operations.

"The goal is simple — give every referee coach in Colorado professional-grade tools, give every club a way to educate their community, and give every player a path into officiating. CREST is how we get there."

— CREST Program, Colorado Soccer Association
